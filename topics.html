The Big Arc

2019 — What is intelligence? How do perception, modeling, decision-making, and execution work as a unified system?

2022–2023 — Independence as financial control. Systematic innovation. Critical thinking frameworks. Ideas are free; execution is everything.

2024–2025 — AI governance. Constitutional design from first principles. Systems serving insiders, not people. Ancient emperors as a template for governing superintelligent AI.

2026 — Building AI agents as partners. Multi-agent architecture. Memory separation. CI/CD for AI gateways. The wisdom stack: principles finally enforced by code, not willpower.

The thread: Designing governance systems — whether for code, AI agents, or nations — that make complex, powerful entities behave correctly through structural architecture rather than trust or force.
