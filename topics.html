The Big Arc

2019 — What is intelligence? How do perception, modeling, decision-making, and execution work?

2022–2023 — Independence = financial control. Systematic innovation. Critical thinking frameworks.

2024–2025 — AI governance. Constitutional design from first principles. Systems serving insiders, not people.

2026 — Building AI agents that work as partners. Multi-agent architecture. "Your AI should not be your autocrat."

The thread: Designing governance systems — whether for code, AI agents, or nations — that make complex, powerful entities behave correctly through structural architecture rather than trust or force.
